About Us

S&H Global Trading Corp. was established in 2014 by CEO Jeffrey Han, located at 13740 N Highway 183 STE K1, Austin, TX 78750. We prioritize client profitability, ensuring quality assurance and claim resolution.

Vision & Values

Our vision is to be a specialized importer and distributor aligned with current US consumer trends.

We deal in eco-friendly foods, household goods, cosmetics, office supplies, leisure products, security devices, IT consumables, and more.

Our future strategy involves establishing and strengthening new import and distribution channels across the US.

We seek to franchise excellent quality products to serve the growing market.

Business Types

  • Import-Distribution: Providing clients profit centers and ensuring quality assurance through expert import-distribution services.
  • Business Consulting: Offering specialist consulting for foreign companies establishing US entities and market entry strategies.

Products & Strategy

  • Household & Lifestyle Products: High-efficiency daily goods, trendy design, direct sourcing for cost savings.
  • Cosmetics & Beauty: Global functional products, safety-certified lines, and authenticity guarantees.
  • Office Supplies & IT Consumables: Office equipment and accessories, innovative designs, bulk purchase competitiveness.
  • Leisure & Security Products: Lifestyle gadgets, security devices, durability verified, flexible inventory management.
